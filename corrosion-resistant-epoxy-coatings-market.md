<p><p>Corrosion-resistant epoxy coatings are designed to protect surfaces from deteriorating due to environmental factors. They come in two primary types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based epoxies typically offer strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications. However, they contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based epoxies are more environmentally friendly and easier to apply, with lower VOC levels. They are increasingly popular for residential and commercial uses, balancing performance with environmental considerations.</p></p>

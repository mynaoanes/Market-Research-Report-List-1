<p><p>The Metal Noise Barrier market features two primary types: Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers and Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers. Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers are installed directly on the ground and are typically used along highways or railways to mitigate sound pollution from traffic. In contrast, Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers are attached to existing structures, such as buildings or bridges, addressing noise issues in urban areas without extensive land use. Both types aim to enhance acoustic comfort and reduce noise impact in populated regions.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Instrument Detection</li><li>Reagent Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protein detection and quantitation market is divided into two primary types: instrument detection and reagent detection. Instrument detection involves the use of advanced equipment, such as spectrophotometers, mass spectrometers, and chromatographs, to identify and measure proteins based on their physical and chemical properties. In contrast, reagent detection relies on specialized chemicals and kits, such as antibody-based assays and fluorescent dyes, to facilitate the identification and quantification of proteins in various samples, often providing simpler and quicker results.</p></p>

<p><p>The Single-Mode Fiber Optic Connectors market includes various types such as ST Simplex, FC Simplex, and SC Simplex connectors. ST Simplex connectors feature a bayonet-style coupling mechanism and are widely used in networking applications. FC Simplex connectors utilize a threaded coupling for a stable connection, ideal for high-performance environments. SC Simplex connectors are known for their push-pull design, facilitating easy connections in dense network setups. Each type serves specific needs in telecommunications, data centers, and broader networking industries.</p></p>

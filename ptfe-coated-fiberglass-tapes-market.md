<p><p>PTFE coated fiberglass tapes are categorized into silicone and acrylic market types based on their adhesive properties and applications. Silicone-based tapes are known for their high-temperature resistance, making them suitable for applications in extreme environments, often used in electrical insulation and heat-resistant surfaces. On the other hand, acrylic-based tapes provide strong adhesion and excellent durability, ideal for general-purpose applications where long-term bonding and resistance to weathering are essential. Both types serve industries ranging from manufacturing to aerospace.</p></p>

<p><p>The video door system market comprises two primary types: Wi-Fi type and Radio Frequency (RF) type. Wi-Fi video door systems use internet connectivity to transmit video and audio, allowing users to interact remotely via smartphones or apps. These systems offer flexibility and ease of installation but rely on network stability. In contrast, Radio Frequency types operate on dedicated RF signals, ensuring reliable communication within a limited range. They often feature lower latency and independence from internet connectivity, making them suitable for remote areas.</p></p>

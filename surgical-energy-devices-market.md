<p><p>The surgical energy devices market encompasses various technologies used to enhance surgical procedures. Monopolar devices use a single probe to deliver energy, allowing for cutting and coagulation. Bipolar devices utilize two electrodes for localized energy delivery, minimizing damage to surrounding tissue. Ultrasonic devices generate high-frequency vibrations to dissect and coagulate tissues simultaneously. Other devices might include laser and thermal technologies, each providing unique advantages in precision, efficiency, and safety during surgeries, catering to diverse surgical needs and specialties.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Manual Heat Press</li><li>Semi-automatic Heat Press</li><li>Automatic Heat Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat press market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic heat presses. Manual heat presses require full operator control for temperature and pressure settings, making them ideal for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic heat presses offer a blend of manual and automatic features, streamlining some processes while still requiring operator input. Automatic heat presses are fully mechanized, providing consistent results with minimal human intervention, making them suitable for large-scale production and efficient commercial applications.</p></p>

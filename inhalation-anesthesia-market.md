<p><p>The inhalation anesthesia market comprises three primary agents: Sevoflurane, Desflurane, and Isoflurane. Sevoflurane is favored for its rapid onset and minimal respiratory irritation, making it suitable for outpatient procedures. Desflurane, characterized by its fast recovery profile, is often used in procedures requiring quick patient turnover. Isoflurane, with its established efficacy and safety record, is widely utilized in various surgical settings. Together, these agents cater to diverse surgical needs, impacting patient comfort and recovery times in anesthesia practice.</p></p>

<p><p>Waterproof breathable fabrics are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their performance characteristics. Type I fabrics offer basic waterproofing and breathability, suitable for general outdoor activities. They typically feature a durable water-repellent (DWR) finish and a moisture-wicking lining. Type II fabrics, on the other hand, provide enhanced performance, featuring advanced technologies for superior waterproofing and breathability. These are ideal for more intense activities and extreme weather conditions, often incorporating multi-layer constructions for improved durability and comfort.</p></p>

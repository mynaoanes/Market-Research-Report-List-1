<p><ul><li>Air Insulated Switchgears</li><li>Gas Insulated Switchgears</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LV/MV switchgear market consists of three main types: Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS), Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS), and others. AIS uses air as an insulating medium and is popular for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, typically deployed in outdoor environments. GIS employs gas, primarily sulfur hexafluoride, providing a compact solution ideal for limited spaces and urban settings, thanks to its superior insulating properties. Other types include modular and hybrid switchgears, catering to specialized applications and diverse installation requirements.</p></p>

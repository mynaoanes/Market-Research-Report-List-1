<p><strong>The In-Display Fingerprint Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-display fingerprint scanners are classified into three main types: optical, ultrasonic, and capacitive. Optical scanners use light to capture a fingerprint image, offering quick and efficient authentication. Ultrasonic scanners utilize sound waves to map the unique features of a fingerprint, providing enhanced security and performance in various conditions. Capacitive scanners rely on electrical charge differences to identify ridges and valleys in a fingerprint, allowing for accurate detection. Each type offers distinct advantages suited to different devices and user preferences.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Biological Inert Ceramic</li><li>Bioactive Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zirconia Bioceramic market encompasses two primary types: biological inert ceramics and bioactive ceramics. Biological inert ceramics are designed to be non-reactive within the body, providing structural support without interacting with biological tissues, making them suitable for implants. Bioactive ceramics, on the other hand, promote biocompatibility and encourage tissue integration through chemical interactions with surrounding biological environments. Both types play crucial roles in medical applications, particularly in dentistry and orthopedics, enhancing patient outcomes through improved integration and functionality.</p></p>

<p><p>The cardiac biomarker diagnostic test kits market is segmented into several categories based on the biomarkers used for diagnosing cardiac conditions. Troponin test kits are utilized to detect heart muscle damage, particularly after a myocardial infarction. Myoglobin test kits measure the presence of myoglobin in the blood, indicating muscle injury. CK-MB test kits assess creatine kinase levels for heart damage evaluation, while BNP test kits measure B-type natriuretic peptide levels to diagnose heart failure. Other kits may include additional biomarkers for comprehensive cardiac assessment.</p></p>

<p><p>The veterinary dental scalers market is divided into two main types: ultrasonic and mechanical. Ultrasonic scalers utilize high-frequency vibrations to efficiently remove tartar and plaque from animal teeth, offering quicker procedures and reducing the need for manual effort. In contrast, mechanical scalers rely on manual instruments for teeth cleaning, providing a more traditional approach. Both types are essential for maintaining animal dental health, with ultrasonic scalers gaining popularity for their speed and effectiveness in veterinary practices.</p></p>

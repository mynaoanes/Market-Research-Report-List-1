<p><p>The organ preservation techniques market encompasses three primary types: static cold storage, organ preservation fluids, and machine perfusion techniques. Static cold storage involves storing organs in low temperatures with minimal metabolic activity, ensuring viability until transplantation. Organ preservation fluids enhance storage solutions to maintain organ integrity and function during transport. Machine perfusion techniques use specialized devices to continuously supply nutrients and oxygen to organs, improving viability and extending preservation times. Together, these methods are crucial for successful organ transplantation.</p></p>

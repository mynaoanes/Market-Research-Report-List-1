<p><strong>The Peptide Nucleic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Chain</li><li>Single Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peptide Nucleic Acid (PNA) market is primarily divided into two types: double-chain and single-chain. Double-chain PNA consists of two complementary strands that form stable duplexes, enhancing specificity and binding affinity for target sequences, making it suitable for applications like gene detection and diagnosis. In contrast, single-chain PNA features a single strand that can hybridize with target nucleic acids, providing unique advantages in antisense therapy and molecular probes. Both types offer distinct functionalities in genetic research and therapeutic development.</p></p>

<p><p>The non-recirculating air curtains market is segmented into three primary types based on installation methods: horizontal, vertical, and recessed. Horizontal installation involves mounting the air curtain above doorways or openings, creating an airflow barrier that prevents outside air from entering. Vertical installation is used alongside larger openings, directing airflow from the sides. Recessed air curtains are integrated into the ceiling or wall cavities for a streamlined appearance, maintaining aesthetics while providing effective air separation in commercial spaces.</p></p>

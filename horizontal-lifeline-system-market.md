<p><p>A Horizontal Lifeline System is a safety mechanism designed for workers operating at heights, providing continuous fall protection along horizontal surfaces. This system consists of a cable or rope anchored between two or more points, allowing personnel to move freely while being secured to the lifeline. It is widely utilized in various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and maintenance, where workers face fall hazards.</p><p>The Horizontal Lifeline System Market is experiencing significant growth driven by increased safety regulations and the rising focus on worker protection.

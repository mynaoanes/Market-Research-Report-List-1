<p><p>The laser bar code scanner market comprises two main types: portable bar code scanners and fixed mount scanners. Portable bar code scanners are handheld devices that offer flexibility and mobility, allowing users to scan barcodes on-the-go, making them ideal for retail and inventory management. In contrast, fixed mount scanners are stationary devices typically installed at points of sale or conveyor belts, offering high-speed scanning and automation for processing high volumes of items efficiently, enhancing workflow in various industrial applications.</p></p>

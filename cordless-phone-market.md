<p><strong>The Cordless Phone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cordless phone market comprises mainly two types: analog and DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications). Analog cordless phones use traditional radio frequency signals to transmit voice, offering basic functionality but limited security and range. In contrast, DECT phones utilize digital technology, providing improved security, clearer sound quality, and longer range. DECT systems are designed specifically for voice communication, allowing multiple handsets to connect to a single base unit without interference, making them more popular for home and business use.</p></p>

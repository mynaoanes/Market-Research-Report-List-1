<p><strong>The Mucoactive Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expectorants</li><li>Mucolytics</li><li>Mucokinetics</li><li>Mucoregulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mucoactive agents are therapeutics aimed at managing mucus in respiratory conditions. Expectorants help to thin and expel mucus, promoting easier coughing. Mucolytics break down mucus structure, reducing its viscosity. Mucokinetics enhance the movement of mucus through airways, aiding in clearance. Mucoregulators modulate mucus production and secretion, balancing mucus levels to prevent obstruction. Together, these agents support respiratory health by improving airway hygiene and alleviating symptoms associated with conditions like asthma, COPD, and bronchitis.</p></p>

<p><p>The surgical reusable medical gloves market consists primarily of two types: latex and nitrile rubber. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity, fit, and tactile sensitivity, making them ideal for delicate procedures. However, they can cause allergic reactions in some users. Nitrile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, provide greater chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for a wider range of surgical applications. Both types are designed for repeated use, emphasizing safety and environmental sustainability in medical settings.</p></p>

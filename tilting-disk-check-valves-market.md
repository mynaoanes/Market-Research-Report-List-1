<p><p>Tilting disk check valves are available in three main types based on their ends: flanged end, threaded end, and welding end. Flanged end check valves feature flanges for easy installation and removal, commonly used in larger pipelines. Threaded end valves are designed for smaller applications, allowing for straightforward connection through threaded fittings. Welding end valves require welding for installation, ensuring a robust and leak-proof seal, ideal for high-pressure applications. Each type serves specific operational and installation needs in fluid control systems.</p></p>

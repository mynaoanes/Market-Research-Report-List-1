<p><p>The inorganic soil amendments market is categorized into two main types: dry and liquid. Dry amendments, such as limestone and gypsum, are typically granular and applied to improve soil structure, pH levels, and nutrient availability. They have a slow release rate and long-lasting effects. Liquid amendments, on the other hand, are solutions that provide quick nutrient absorption and can enhance soil properties more rapidly. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing soil fertility and optimizing plant growth in various agricultural practices.</p></p>

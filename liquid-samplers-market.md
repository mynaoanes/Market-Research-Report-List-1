<p><strong>The Liquid Samplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Cross Section Sampling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid samplers are devices used to collect samples of fluids for analysis. In the single point sampling market, samples are taken from a specific location, providing data representative of that particular spot, which is useful for targeted assessments. In contrast, cross section sampling involves collecting samples from multiple points across a fluid system, offering a broader overview of the liquid's characteristics. This method is beneficial for understanding variations in composition and quality throughout the entire system.</p></p>

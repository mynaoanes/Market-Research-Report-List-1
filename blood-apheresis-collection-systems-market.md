<p><strong>The Blood Apheresis Collection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blood apheresis collection systems are vital for separating blood components for therapeutic or research purposes. The market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation uses centrifugal force to separate blood components based on density, effectively isolating plasma or cells. Membrane filtration, on the other hand, employs selective permeable membranes to separate components by size and molecular weight, offering a gentler approach that minimizes damage to cellular elements. Both methods are crucial for efficient blood component collection.</p></p>

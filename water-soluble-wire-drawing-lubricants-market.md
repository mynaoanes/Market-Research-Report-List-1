<p><p>Water-soluble wire drawing lubricants are essential for reducing friction during wire production. The market comprises three main types: emulsions, dispersions, and liquid soaps. Emulsions combine oils and water, offering excellent lubrication and cooling properties. Dispersions contain solid lubricating particles suspended in water for enhanced performance in various conditions. Liquid soaps, typically made from fatty acids, provide lubrication while being easy to clean off post-manufacturing. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing wire drawing processes while ensuring surface integrity.</p></p>

<p><p>The Surgical Anesthesia System market can be categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic anesthesia systems. Fully automatic anesthesia systems are advanced devices that autonomously monitor and adjust anesthesia delivery based on patient parameters, ensuring optimal dosing and minimal human intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic anesthesia systems require some manual input from the anesthesiologist to adjust dosages, providing a balance between autonomy and professional oversight. Both types aim to enhance patient safety, improve surgical outcomes, and streamline the anesthesia process.</p></p>

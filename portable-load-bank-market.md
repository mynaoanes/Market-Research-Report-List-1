<p><ul><li>Resistive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive Reactive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable load bank market comprises three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive-reactive load banks. Resistive load banks create electrical loads solely based on resistive components, ideal for testing power supplies. Reactive load banks simulate inductive or capacitive loads, useful for assessing generator performance and stability. Resistive-reactive load banks combine both elements, providing a comprehensive evaluation of power systems under various conditions. Together, these load banks ensure efficient testing and management of electrical systems in diverse applications.</p></p>

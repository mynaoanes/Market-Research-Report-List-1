<p><p>The medical printing film market is categorized into three main types: dry film, thermal film, and B-ultrasound film. Dry film is used for developing radiographic images without the need for wet processing, offering convenience and efficiency. Thermal film utilizes heat to produce high-quality images, primarily in digital radiography systems. B-ultrasound film is specifically designed for capturing ultrasound images, providing clear and precise results essential for diagnostic purposes. Each type caters to distinct medical imaging requirements, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>

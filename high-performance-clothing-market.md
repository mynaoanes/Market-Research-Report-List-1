<p><p>The High Performance Clothing market comprises three primary types: synthetic, cotton, and wool. Synthetic fabrics, such as polyester and nylon, offer superior moisture-wicking and durability, making them ideal for athletic wear. Cotton is breathable and comfortable but may lack moisture management, making it better suited for casual or light activity. Wool, particularly merino, excels in temperature regulation and odor resistance, making it a popular choice for outdoor and endurance activities. Each fabric type caters to specific performance needs in activewear.</p></p>

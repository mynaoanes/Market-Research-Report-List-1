<p><p>Pusher furnaces are essential in heat treatment and metal processing, primarily categorized into flat hearth and tubular hearth types. Flat hearth pusher furnaces feature a level surface for efficient loading and unloading of materials, making them ideal for thin products. In contrast, tubular hearth pusher furnaces utilize a cylindrical design, optimizing space and enhancing heat distribution for bulk materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, improving productivity and energy efficiency in thermal processing applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The exhaust flex pipe market consists of three primary types: unlined, braid lined, and interlock lined. Unlined flex pipes are straightforward designs without reinforcements, suitable for low-stress applications. Braid lined pipes incorporate a braided exterior, enhancing strength and flexibility, making them ideal for high-performance situations. Interlock lined pipes feature an interwoven design that provides superior durability and can withstand extreme conditions, often used in heavy-duty vehicles. Each type serves specific needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>

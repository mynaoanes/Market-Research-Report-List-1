<p><p>The Directed Energy Deposition (DED) 3D printer market features three primary types: laser, electron beam, and plasma arc. Laser DED uses focused beams to melt materials for precise layer formation, suitable for complex geometries. Electron beam DED relies on high-energy beams in a vacuum for superior density and material properties, ideal for aerospace applications. Plasma arc DED utilizes an electric arc to generate heat, allowing for rapid deposition and versatility with various materials, often employed in industrial and repair processes.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Coating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sputtering Deposition</li><li>Evaporation Deposition</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) coating system market is primarily categorized into three types: Sputtering Deposition, Evaporation Deposition, and Others. Sputtering Deposition involves ejected material from a target, which condenses on a substrate, creating a thin film. Evaporation Deposition involves heating materials in a vacuum to produce vapor, which then deposits on surfaces. The "Others" category includes alternative methods like Cathodic Arc Deposition and Ion Plating, offering diverse coating solutions for various applications across industries.</p></p>
